About Us

Our system leverages satellite imagery and Digital Elevation Models (DEMs) to analyze terrain and determine the safest, most efficient routes. Using Machine Learning algorithms, it assesses slope, elevation, and obstacles like rivers or cliffs to optimize navigation. Real-time weather updates enable dynamic route adjustments, making it ideal for off-road travel and disaster response.
